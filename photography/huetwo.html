GREEN

For this project I tried to challenge myself to take all photos from outdoors.

TEXTURE

Green is my favourite colour. The first image I took for the photoshoot was the first image here. It was a box filled with salt. And the sidewalk sole purpose was to lead a person to that very box. It was this kind of simplicity that made me grin. It really set the tone for me for the entire photoshoot. And this simplicity was something I tried to encompass in all my images.

SHAPE

I was really hoping that I could find more shots of nature for shapes but I only found one. The evergreen tree is really the only last beauty of nature before the snow takes over. As our days gets darker and grayer, the ever-green tree continues to fight mother nature to express itself.

TINT

Thankfully, a forest is behind our campus, and the last picutre shows the chaos between the fence(urban expansion) and the trees(natures regression). It is something that is in constant battle with each other and my photos is a reflection of that, less than half of my green pictures are actually shots of nature.
