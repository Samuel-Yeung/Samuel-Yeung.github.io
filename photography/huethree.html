BROWN

For this project I tried to challenge myself to take all photos from outdoors.

TEXTURE

The colour brown is an interesting colour. It is such a prominent colour in nature. From the leaves, to dirt, to rocks, it is all around us but sadly people see it as a disgusting colour.

SHAPE

I see a continued theme in all three pictures. The emotion of isolation, like how the manhole is by itself as the grass slowly swallows it up. The pine, lonely as it surrounded by green pines. And the fallen leaf, which had so much grace and now vulnerable.

TINT

A colour of brown that is quite interesting is rust. Natures small rebellion in urban society. Rust changes the colour of objects and decreases the strength of objects. And these three images are great example of rust and errosion
