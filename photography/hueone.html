BLUE

For this project I tried to challenge myself to take all photos from outdoors.

TEXTURE

Urban and grudgy was the emotion I was trying to express here. Blue textures are hard to come by outside. I thought this might be easier since one of the UOIT's offical colours are Blue. But this was harder to find, especially for textures.

SHAPE

Cylinders and more circles. I guess these are the objects, found most easily around campus. I especially like the photo beer bottle, even though it was littered by someone, it shows that someone had a great time. I can imagine the person, drinking their beer with friends walking in the allyway laughing after a long night of partying. Which implies their slogan, "Up for Whatever"

TINT

For Tint, I tried to find more fades of blue. This is hard to come by other than the blue sky. So most I was limited to digital tints like the first image.
