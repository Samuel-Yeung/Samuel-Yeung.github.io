DESIGN

Typography

After much consideration of font families, I have decided to use a famous font, Roboto, but a variation, Slab. Roboto is an important font as it is made by Google and Google will be implementing this font with Material Design in all of Google's products. To accompany this heavy weighted font, I used a very thin font of Josefin Sans Light. Used primary for text it matches well with Roboto Slab.

Colour

My colour palatte for this site is neutral, this means black, white and mostly grey is used. I wanted the pictures in my site to provide the coloured emotions here, this is a photography and therefore the photos should have every oppurtunity to express its emotion. Headers for pages of a specific hue reflects the colour used, which gives a subtle hint for any user what kind of colour the pictures are expressing without having to read the word.

Layout

Using the layout that was provided, I changed most elements. Gone was the seperator between each header image. The navigation is placed above the header image for easy access. All text are now centre aligned as it present a feel of symmetry amoung the entire site. Header images also display images of symmetry except for this page.
